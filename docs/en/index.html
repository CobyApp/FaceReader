▌ MONSTER ASSOCIATION ARCHIVE ▌

What's your face's
disaster level?

Snap a selfie. Apple Intelligence files you in the One-Punch Man-style Monster Association archive, grades you on a 5-tier scale — Wolf, Tiger, Demon, Dragon, God — and prints a retro VHS wanted poster.

▸ Features
🎯

Face ratio measurement

Vision's landmark AI extracts eye / nose / mouth / face-length ratios. All numbers stay internal to the Association.

🤖

AI villain naming

On-device Apple Intelligence generates an in-universe codename and a two-sentence dossier instantly.

🎴

Wanted poster output

Fixed 1080×1920px canvas, shareable straight to Instagram/TikTok stories. VHS-horror aesthetic baked in.

🛟

Offline fallback

For LLM-unsupported devices or generation failures, a hand-curated library of 100 monsters per language is bundled.

🌐

Three languages

Korean, Japanese, and English UI and posters — fully localized, switchable any time from settings.

🔒

Privacy-first

Photos and AI inference all stay on-device. No account, no tracking, no third-party SDKs.

▸ Five Disaster Tiers

The Association's threat scale mirrors One-Punch Man's hero response classes. God-class is, effectively, the end of the world.

▸ How it works
1

Snap a selfie

Open the app and frame your face inside the on-screen guide. One shot is enough.

2

Grade decided

Vision reads your face ratios and slots you into one of the five disaster tiers.

3

Filed as a monster

Apple Intelligence drafts your codename and dossier. You are now an officially registered fictional menace.

4

Share the poster

Save the wanted poster and compete with friends on social media for highest disaster level.

▸ Privacy
  • Camera images are processed on-device and never sent to a server.
  • Apple Intelligence generation runs entirely on-device.
  • No accounts required — no email, no password, no signup.
  • No tracking, ads, or third-party analytics of any kind.
  • Saved photos and posters stay in your device storage only.
